About Me

My journey goes back to the early 2000's Myspace days where I used to get lost for hours designing layouts

In May of 2022, I decided to embark on the journey of teaching myself JavaScript and the many other technologies that go into creating an amazing user experience.

My goal is to join a team of self-starters and build amazing things together
